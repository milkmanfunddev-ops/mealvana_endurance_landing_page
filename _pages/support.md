## Common Questions

### How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password from the login screen by tapping "Forgot Password" and following the instructions sent to your email.

### How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at [support@mealvana.io](mailto:support@mealvana.io) with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

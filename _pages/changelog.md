# Changelog

Stay up to date with the latest improvements to Mealvana Endurance.

<br>

### `Latest`
# **Version 1.15**
A major update rebuilding the nutrition plan experience from the ground up — redesigned during-activity nutrition, smarter food placement, and a new template-based pre-workout system.

#### What's New
- **Redesigned During-Activity Nutrition** — Completely overhauled food selection with a new timeline UI and hour-by-hour nutrition view so you can see exactly what to eat and when
- **Template-Based Pre-Workout Meals** — New template system for pre-workout food planning with smart beverage handling and fluid tracking
- **Drag-to-Place Food Items** — Intuitive drag-to-hour and tap-to-place model lets you position nutrition items exactly where you want them
- **Global Sip Section** — Dedicated section for sip-based items like sports drinks and water with inline quantity adjustment
- **Custom Nutrition Targets** — Manually adjust your carb, protein, and hydration targets to match your personal preferences
- **Smart Pre-Activity Fueling** — Duration-aware fueling window with smarter recommendations for shorter vs longer activities
- **Post-Workout Carb Feedback** — Track actual vs planned carb intake to improve future recommendations
- **Improved Brick Workout Nutrition** — Full multi-sport support in the V2 nutrition engine with transition phase planning

#### Bug Fixes
- Fixed multiple layout and data display issues in the by-hour nutrition view
- Fixed water filtering, sodium cap, and variety selection during activity planning
- Improved version check handling for smoother app updates
- Fixed template food preference handling and fluid tracking
- Various stability and performance improvements

### `Brick Workouts`
# **Version 1.14.2**
A major feature release introducing brick workouts, an upgraded nutrition algorithm, and improved sync reliability.

#### What's New
- **Brick Workouts** — Combine swimming, cycling, and running into a single multi-sport training session with unified nutrition planning and transition period support
- **Drag-to-Reorder** — Arrange your sport segments in any order and see per-phase nutrition breakdowns on the activity detail screen
- **Multi-Sport Calendar Indicators** — Easily identify brick workouts on your activities calendar at a glance
- **Nutrition Algorithm v3** — Completely rewritten macro generation engine with more accurate carbohydrate, protein, and hydration recommendations
- **Schema Recovery Service** — Automatic detection and recovery from database issues without losing your workout history
- **Improved Training Platform Sync** — Better reliability and immediate cloud backup when syncing activities from Training Peaks and Final Surge

#### Bug Fixes
- Fixed duplicate activities when syncing with Training Peaks or Final Surge
- Corrected three nutrition algorithm bugs — field name mismatch, missing brick sweat data, and fallback formula accuracy
- Fixed navigation and layout issues for brick workout activities
- Improved error handling and provider stability throughout the app
- Various performance and stability improvements

### `Initial Release`
# **Version 1.0**
The first public release of Mealvana Endurance — your AI-powered nutrition companion for endurance athletes.

#### Features
- **Personalized Nutrition Plans** — Science-based recommendations using ACSM formulas tailored to your body weight, distance, and pace
- **Multi-Sport Support** — Track running, cycling, and swimming activities
- **Food Preferences** — Like or dislike foods to customize your nutrition plans
- **Activities Calendar** — Schedule and manage your workouts
- **Events & Races** — Add upcoming races with goal times and automatic nutrition planning
- **Carb Loading Planner** — 1-7 day carb loading protocols for race preparation
- **Offline Mode** — Full functionality without internet connection
- **Apple & Google Sign-In** — Optional account creation for data backup across devices
